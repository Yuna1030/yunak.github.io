概略/About

I am interested in how social relationships such as parent-child, family, school/peers, community, and country are biologically embedded within individuals and make people who they are, and in turn how individual presentations are externalized into the relationships. I am also interested in how these interdependent relationships continue with dynamic changes throughout life, across multiple generations, and even historically.
Ultimately, I hope to uncover the nature of society in which each individual can realize their full capability and live their own life.

多くの人が、健康で自分らしく生きられる社会を実現するため、学び、研究しています。

Keywords: social epidemiology; social relationships; child and adolescent; mental health; population neuroscience
